"Creative Thinking – Ideation" workshop at IIT Kanpur

The Syndicate Bank Entrepreneurship Research and Training Centre (SBERTC) at Indian Institute of Technology, Kanpur kicks off its first ever workshop on “Creative thinking - Ideation” for Kanpur school teachers. The workshop was conducted by Dr Ashutosh Khanna of International Management Institute, New Delhi and Dr Deepu Philip of the Department of Industrial and Management Engineering, IIT Kanpur. It was attended by 23 teachers from 10 schools, and was spread over two days from May 12 – 13, 2016.


The workshop was an exciting blend of traditional learning with many hands-on exercises, real life problem solving, interactive sessions, puzzles, quizzes and games which left the participants energised and rich with skills on how to manoeuvre school children from all socio-economic backgrounds to think outside the box and nurture their unconventional talents and skills.


The workshop followed a systematic approach on how to introduce “Ideation” at the basic level in schools, imparting teachers with interactive and experiential tools and techniques to do so, and breaking away from traditional teaching methods.


Based on the feedback received from the current workshop, IIT Kanpur plans to tailor its future workshops in ways which would be contributing to the upliftment of the society and ultimately the economic status of the country.

Shri Shakti Degree College

Shri Shakti Degree College is owned and controlled by Shri Shakti Shikshan Sansthan, a society registered under Societies Registration Act, 1860 (Reg. No. 1418/22-06-07). The foundation brick was laid down by Shri R. C. Trivedi (Ex Director of Bank of Baroda) on 8th Aug. 2002 in a village of remote area being his native place. The college is spread over 20,000 sq. meter area with attractive greenery inside the college and with completely pollution free, calm and quiet environmental situation decorated by outside greenery of agricultural crops.


The college is situated in the village of Snakhahari, post- Harbaspur, Tehsil- Ghatampur, District- Knapur Nagar-209206 (U. P.) on Ghatampur – Amauli Road at the distance of 13 Km From Ghatampur and 12 KM from Amauli where frequent private buses and tempoes are available to reach the College.


Institution is registered under section 2(f) and 12(B) of UGC act ,1956.
