Workshops

We provide state of the art training programs both at elementary and advanced level for students & faculties and existing & potential entrepreneurs. Beneficiaries include school going children and also school teachers. These are one time events that may provide opportunity to the host institutions to further leverage the knowledge base of SBERTC to create a whole entrepreneurial ecosystem at their institution. These workshops act as awareness cum stimulation to ignite and inspire the potential entrepreneurs and sail through the challenges of their entrepreneurial journey.
All government, Industry associations, self help groups, social & women specific entrepreneurship development bodies, organisations working for rural entrepreneurship development are encouraged to write to us for organising workshops with us.
